About Us

The Brisbane Dog Training Club Inc, Queensland's longest-running dog training club since 1961, helps owners build better relationships with their dogs through fun, practical training.

Training keeps your dog active, improves behaviour, and connects you with a community of like-minded owners. Our volunteer trainers bring experience across many breeds and activities, including agility, scent work, tricks, and more.
