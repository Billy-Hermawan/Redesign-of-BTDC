Puppy Preschool

Our Puppy Preschool is designed to help your pup build confidence and good habits from the start. With small, rolling classes, they'll get to socialise with different puppies and people each week.

We focus on basic manners and essential life skills like sit, recall, loose leash walking, and calm behaviour around others, all taught through simple, reward-based training.
